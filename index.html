Education and Care

KIDS Special Education School will find an approach to your child

KIDS is a special education school that has been operating since 2014. The teachers of our school have a huge experience working with children with special needs. We use the latest learning methods that have proven their effectiveness. We create all the necessary conditions so that every child can receive knowledge in a safe and comfortable environment.

Our History

September 2014

After graduating from Drew University with a master's degree in Montessori pedagogy, the principal of our school opened a small study group in which children with special needs could learn new things in a playful way.

March 2015

Our kids club grew and expanded. The question arose about opening a full-fledged school. Thanks to the parents and authorities of New York City, a special education school has emerged in 2000 and accepted the first 20 students.

May 2021

Due to the growing number of students, the school went through a major reorganization. Our school has moved to a new building, the staff of teachers has expanded. The school was already attended by 117 students.
